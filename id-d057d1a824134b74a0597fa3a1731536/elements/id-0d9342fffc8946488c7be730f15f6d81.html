Situations and Geofencing data sharing ()
Road owners and regulators need to inform the road users on deviating Situations on the roads. That could be that a road is closed or road work is ongoing. Geographic areas (geofences) could also be temporarily regulated, for instance extra rush hour cost, or some benefits of using zero-emitting transport in an area. Situations and Geofences defined in an area could be shared to 3rd party actors. Use in LL and sim: Used in Herøya Living Lab to handle deviations for Fleet Management System.
