Network User ()
The Network User role is responsible for the operation of the vehicle/vessel, integration in the traffic in compliance with laws and regulations and for safety issues related to behaviour and operation of the vehicle/vessel The role may be covered by a person supported by systems (e.g. a driver, pedestrian or a cyclist) or a systems (in the the vehicle/vessel). Is the case for Connected and Autoomated Vehicles (CAVs), the role is covered by systems. Laws and regulations must be followed.
