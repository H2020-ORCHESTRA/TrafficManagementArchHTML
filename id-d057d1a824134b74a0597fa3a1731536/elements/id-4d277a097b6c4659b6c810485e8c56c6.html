Activity registration ()
Connecting manually driven vehicles to the Fleet Management System to document where and what the vehicle is doing. Data used for real-time monitoring and reporting. An app transmitting the phone’s position while activated. Toggle-buttons activated by the driver to register different types of work performed. Use in LL and sim: Used in Herøya Living Lab to get insight when and where the Guiding vehicle is guiding others or not.
