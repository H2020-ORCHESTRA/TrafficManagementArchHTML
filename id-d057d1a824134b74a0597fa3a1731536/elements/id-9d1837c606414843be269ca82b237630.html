Traffic Orchestrator ()
The Traffic Orchestrator role is responsible for - Traffic management. This is the management of the traffic flow in the networl to arrange for safety, efficienccy and optimal utilisation of the network. - Transport network resource management. This is the management of the access to and use of limited resources like parking spaces, loading bays, waiting areas, etc. - Coordination towards the traffic orchestration in other modes, networks and/or governance areas and thereby to contrinute to a more optimal transport system as a whole - across modes and governance areas. The aim is to arrange for sustainable transport from an environmental, economical and societal point of view.
