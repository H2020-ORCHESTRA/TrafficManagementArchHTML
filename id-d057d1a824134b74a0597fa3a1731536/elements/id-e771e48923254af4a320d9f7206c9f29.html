Auction Mechanism for conflict resolution ()
Distributed single-round auction to arbitrate in case of conflicting trucks in congested time-windows and determine port access in merit-based manner. The auction will use the tradable credit as currency. Use in LL and sim: The distributed auction will act as a tool to escape infeasibility between conflicting truck, competing for the same slots, in the block-based scheduler
