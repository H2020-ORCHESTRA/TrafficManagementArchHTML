Operation Plan ()
This is the plan addressing current and upcomming scheduled traffic events in the area of governance related to approved transport operations. This may for example be entries/exits of transport means to/from the area and other planned actions taken by the transport means (waiting and other stops, loading/unloading at waypoints, etc.). The operation plan will link to the transport operations business objects where more details on the operations can be found.
